Foundations

Founded amongst the rubble of the post-pandemic wastelands, Seal Team Six was formed out of a mutual interest in survival RPG games.
Before the Team was formed, it started off as a group of random dudes playing the same game at the right moment. The group quickly banded together
and began a journey together that has spanned universes.

Even when the realities of life have shifted our priorities, we still manage to find the time to come together.
